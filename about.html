About Us

Akron's #1 Urban Hair Salon

Established in 2006, Beauty Inc-Urban Hair Retreat quickly built a stellar reputation for being the premier beauty salon for African American hair in Akron Ohio. We take much pride in the work we do. We know how important it is for women to look and feel their best and we are committed to providing services that deliver the best results with each and every client we serve; We offer natural hair care, hair color, relaxers, sew ins, precision haircuts, and other hair and beauty salon services. We take pride in the quality of services we provide. We have trained long and hard in order to come up with exceptional results.

Ask any of our many customers and they'll tell you that they come to us on a regular basis because they love the feeling of security they get every time they walk through our doors. Our customers know that no matter how stressed or tired-looking they may have been when they came in, that they'll leave out looking and feeling fabulous!
